Summer Bucket List

Working on this project taught me how to embed images to appear at different times. This program helps users decide what to do in their free time. They can opt for a surprise random activity or apply parameters, such as location, to specify the activity they would like. It is called Summer Bucket List, because it's a list of activities for users to try, particularly in the summer!
